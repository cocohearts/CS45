Starcraft 2 Terran Bio

Introduction

Starcraft 2 is a real-time strategy game. In such a genre of game, players mine resources, build armies, and attack each other in intense games that can last anywhere from 5 minutes to an hour, from start to finish. A composition in Starcraft is a collection of units that work well together and complement each other.

According to Starcraft lore, there are three races competing for control of the galaxy. The Terrans are the humans, and one frequently seen composition is called Terran bio. Bio refers to the biological units of the Terran race, namely the marine and the marauder.

Here are images of the marine and the marauder.

The important things to know about the marine are:

  • Cheap
  • Ranged
  • Small hit box
  • Small HP pool
  • Vulnerable to splash damage
  • Attacks quickly, so affected very much by upgrades

The important things to know about the marauder are:

  • Good deal more expensive than marine
  • Ranged
  • Bonus damage against armored
  • Had large HP pool and medium hit box, so good for soaking up splash damage
  • Doesn't attack as quickly as the marine

Upgrades and Upgrade Theory

The Marine and Marauder benefit from important upgrades. Combat Shield gives Marines an extra 10 HP, and Stimpack gives biological units the ability to sacrifice some health for a temporary boost in movement speed and attack speed. There is one additional unit, the Medivac, that can heal Bio units, and that is included in the image at the bottom of the page.

Upgrades are a very important part of Starcraft, and Terran bio in particular. The full list of Terran upgrades can be found here.

When an upgrade finishes, it applies to all of your standing units at the same time. This is very important; for Terran Bio, upgrades affect units even more, so Terran Bio gets a massive power spike when upgrades finishes. Thus, Terran Bio build orders center around attacking exactly when some important upgrade finishes, such as Combat/Stim or +2/+2.
